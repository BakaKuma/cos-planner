## 2. What Data the App Accesses
### Google Account Information
When you sign in with Google, the App receives basic profile information provided by Google OAuth:
- Your name
- Your email address
- Your Google account ID
  This information is used solely to authenticate you and is not stored on any external server controlled by the developer.
### Google Drive Data
The App requests access to Google Drive in order to store and retrieve your cosplay project data (such as project notes, item lists, measurements, shops, and shooting locations). Specifically:
- The App creates and reads files in your Google Drive that belong to the App.
- No other files in your Google Drive are accessed.
- Your data remains in your own Google Drive account and is not transmitted to or stored on any server controlled by the developer.
---
## 3. How Your Data Is Used
Your data is used exclusively to provide the core functionality of the App:
- Saving and loading your cosplay projects, items, measurements, shops, and shooting locations
- Synchronizing your data across devices via your own Google Drive
  Your data is **never**:
- Sold to third parties
- Shared with advertisers
- Used for analytics or tracking purposes
- Stored on servers controlled by the developer

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion
All your data is stored in your own Google Drive account. You have full control over it:
- You can delete the App's files directly from Google Drive at any time.
- You can revoke the App's access to your Google account at any time via [Google Account Permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions).
- Uninstalling the App does not automatically delete files from your Google Drive. You can delete them manually.
---

## 6. Data Security
The App does not operate its own server or database. All data is stored in your Google Drive account, which is protected by Google's security infrastructure and your Google account credentials.

---
## 7. Children's Privacy
The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has used the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.
